# 🎮 Reach the Goal

**Reach the Goal** is a 2D grid-based survival game built with OpenGL. The objective is to avoid incoming thorns, collect bonus gems, and reach the top of the arena while surviving increasingly difficult obstacles.

## Features

*  15 × 15 grid-based arena
*  Four-direction player movement
*  Thorns spawn every second and move horizontally & vertically
*  Thorn speed increases over time
*  Four movable shield walls for protection
*  Staying behind a shield wall for more than 3 seconds results in Game Over
*  3-life health system
*  Random Energy Gems worth **+5** points
*  Reach the top of the arena to win
*  Adjustable camera movement

## Scoring

* Move to a new grid: **+1**
* Collect an Energy Gem: **+5**
* Reach the goal: **+10**

## Game Over

* Lose all 3 lives after thorn collisions.
* Stay behind a shield wall for more than 3 seconds.

## Technologies Used

* OpenGL
* GLUT
